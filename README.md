# Marketing A/B Testing

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the results of a marketing A/B experiment to determine whether a new campaign significantly outperforms the control group.

The analysis combines exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical hypothesis testing, and business interpretation to support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 📂 Repository Structure

- `01_data_preparation_eda.ipynb` — data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis.
- `02_ab_testing.ipynb` — statistical hypothesis testing and experiment evaluation.

---

## 🧠 Project Workflow

1. Loaded and explored the experimental dataset.
2. Cleaned and validated the data.
3. Performed exploratory data analysis.
4. Compared the control and treatment groups.
5. Tested statistical assumptions.
6. Conducted hypothesis testing.
7. Interpreted the business impact of the experiment.

## 📊 Results

The project demonstrates a complete A/B testing workflow, from data preparation and exploratory analysis to statistical validation and business interpretation of experimental results.

The final conclusions are based on statistical evidence rather than observed differences alone, supporting informed decision-making in marketing optimization.
